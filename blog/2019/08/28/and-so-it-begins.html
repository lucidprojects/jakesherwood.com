28 Aug 2019 - Jake Sherwood

And So It Begins…

As they often say, today is the first day of the rest of your life… And in that vein today was a big day in the beginning of the rest of my life…

Today was my first day at ITP. It was only orientation but the melting pot of people, enthusiasm, creativity and hunger to change the world was contagious.

Its official I’m an NYU student!

It wasn’t easy getting myself here. Huge life changes, leaving my comfort zone of a stable job, uprooting my family, packing up my home, and moving across country with a toddler. I feel like I’ve been going 24/7 for months.

I guess it was just an intro course to Grad School life…

Excited to see what happens over the next 2 years and what the future holds.

I never in a million years thought I would be back in NYC. Or that I would be going to Grad School. But here I am…

Bring it on ITP! I’m ready to dive in!!!

First skate to School


That’s me!

Huge thanks to my amazingly supportive wife, my parents and my awesome older brother who pushed me to follow in his foot steps and attend ITP ever since the day he got accepted over 7 years ago.

All of whom I would not be here without their help, love and support.

I love you guys!
